# Goat Payments Design System

## Brand Overview
GOAT Payments provides transparent, scalable payment solutions for SMBs.  
**Tagline**: Optimizing payment solutions for SMB’s.

**Personality**: Professional yet approachable, trustworthy, efficient, premium.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Green)**: #96C100 — CTAs, accents, icons
- **Dark Neutral**: #474749 — Headers, body text
- **White**: #FFFFFF — Backgrounds

## Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Extra Bold
- **Body**: Montserrat Regular
- **Accent/Logo**: Oligopoly Regular / Bold

## Logo Rules
- Use the primary logo provided
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Green version on dark backgrounds, white/green on light

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous white space
- Clean hierarchy with strong headings
- Max content width: 1200px

## Voice & Tone
Clear, confident, benefit-driven. Focus on transparency, ease, and growth for SMB merchants.
