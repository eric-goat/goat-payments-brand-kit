# Goat Payments Design System

## Brand Overview
**GOAT Payments** — Optimizing payment solutions for SMBs.
Transparent, scalable payment processing that grows with businesses of all sizes.

**Mission**: Provide transparent processing solutions which make a material impact to our merchant partners around the globe.
**Core Values**: Experience. Integrity. Options.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: #96C100 — CTAs, accents, icons
- **Dark Gray**: #474749 — Headers, body text, dark mode
- **Deep Navy**: #003568 — Authority surfaces, trust accents, dark sections
- **White**: #FFFFFF — Backgrounds, light mode

## Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Extra Bold
- **Body Text**: Montserrat Regular
- **Logo & Accents**: Oligopoly Regular / Bold

## Logo Guidelines
- Primary Logo: "GOAT" (white with green A) + "PAYMENTS" (green)
- Use correct color variations per background
- Maintain clear space requirements
- Do not distort, recolor, or recreate with text

## Layout & Spacing
- Generous white space (minimum 32-64px between sections)
- Clean hierarchy with strong Montserrat headings
- Card style: Subtle shadow, rounded corners, clean borders
- Max content width: 1200px for 1-pagers

## Components
- Buttons: Montserrat Bold, #96C100 background, white text, rounded
- Heroes: Large headline + subheadline + strong green CTA
- Icons: Simple, modern, green accent color

## Voice & Tone
Clear, confident, and helpful. Focus on transparency, ease of use, and real business impact for SMB merchants. Use "you" language.

## Usage Rules
- Consistent across all GTM assets (1-pagers, pitch decks, battlecards, one-sheets)
- Print-ready and web-responsive
- Include logo, tagline, and contact in footers
